Action Required

You must manually add a file upload question to the form.

  1. Click the plus (+) button on the right to add a question.
  2. Choose "File upload" as the question type.
  3. Click "Continue" to acknowledge that form respondents will need to log in.
  4. Click the switch besides "Required" to make this question mandatory.
  5. Keep the maximum number of files to 1 at one and do not restrict on file types.
  6. Change the maximum file size to 1 GB.
  7. Where it says "This form can accept up to 1 GB of files.", click "Change". This will take you to the settings screen.
  8. Under file upload, change the total size limit to 10GB.
